Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100 grms of plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 Ml of milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • lemon wedges
  • caster sugar

Cooking Steps

  1. put eggs, flour and milk in a bowl and whisk
  2. set aside for 30 minutes
  3. set a frying pan over medium heat
  4. cook for 1 minute each side
  5. serve with Lemon wedges and sugar
